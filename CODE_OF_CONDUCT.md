# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, productive experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be direct and constructive in technical discussions
- Critique ideas, not people
- Accept that reasonable people reach different conclusions from the same data
- Give credit where it's due
- Keep discussions on-topic

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissing contributors based on identity rather than substance
- Sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

To report an incident, open a private issue or contact the maintainers directly.
